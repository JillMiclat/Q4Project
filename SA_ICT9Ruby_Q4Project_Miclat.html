What is a Millimeter ?

Millimeter is the metric unit of length used for measuring small objects and quantities such as medicines, length of a pen tip, etc. It is most commonly written as "mm". The word also meaning thousandth of meters.


Solving Manually? (Formulas)

mm: millimeters, cm: centimeters, m: meters, km: kilometers, in: inches

Centimeters cm = mm ÷ 10
Meters m = mm ÷ 1000
Kilometers km = mm ÷ 1000000
Inches cm = mm ÷ 25.4

Examples

Centimeters 101 mm ÷ 10 = 10.1 cm
Meters 101 mm ÷ 1000 = 0.101 m
Kilometers 101 mm ÷ 10 = 0.000101 km
Inches 101 mm ÷ 25.4 = 3.98 in
